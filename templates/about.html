Catherine Giugno

Catherine Giugno is well aware that her majors: English and Computer science are about as similar as Jigglypuff and Bagon. But like both of the little Pokemon creatures, they have one connection: video games. Ever since she first set eyes on a console in 2007, she fell in love with the immersive worlds and complex characters that could somehow inhabit something as two dimensional as a screen. Since then--and diving into the universes of Pokemon, Marvel Strike Force, Fire Emblem, Don't Starve, and Overwatch--she's channeled that fascination into her own writing, creating stories about both the worlds she's loved and those of her own.
